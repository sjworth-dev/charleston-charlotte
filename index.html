Pro Tips

Insider knowledge for the best travel experience

Best Time to Leave

Depart mid-morning (9-10 AM) to avoid both cities' rush hours and arrive before evening traffic.

Save on Gas

South Carolina gas is typically $0.20-0.30 cheaper per gallon. Fill up before the NC border.

Halfway Stop

Columbia (1.5 hrs in) is perfect for a break. Try the Vista district for food and coffee.

Avoid Friday PM

Friday 3-7 PM is the worst time to leave Charleston. Add 30-60 minutes to your trip.
